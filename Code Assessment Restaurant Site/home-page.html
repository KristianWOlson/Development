About the Rusty Dab

In 1902 Morris Olson founded a small tavern on the shores of lake Superior to provide local fishermen with hearty meals and refreshing drinks. Known for its distinctive maritime atmosphere and fish pies, The Rusty Dab has been a staple of the community providing a menu focused on local seafood and navel strength drinks, fit for a sailor. True to its roots, nothing on the menue is fancy, the core clientele being a bit rusty themselves.
